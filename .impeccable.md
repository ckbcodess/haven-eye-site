# Design Context: Haven Eye

### Users
- **Who**: Residents of Ridge seeking professional eye care, ranging from parents with young children to adults needing specialized treatments.
- **Context**: Users want to quickly find services, trust the expertise of the clinic, and book an appointment with minimal friction.
- **Emotional Goal**: Clinical confidence and straightforward ease.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Clinical, Modern, Straightforward.
- **Tone**: Honest, clear, and high-end without being unapproachable.
- **3-Word Personality**: Clinical, Modern, Straightforward.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Airy Editorial Modernism.
- **Theme**: Light Mode / Neutral-First.
- **Hierarchy**: High-contrast typography pairing (Instrument Serif display with Outfit sans-serif body).
- **Differentiation Anchor**: "Surgical whitespace" and a hyper-neutral palette that uses the brand blue solely for primary action anchors.

### Design Principles
1. **Ruthless Clarity**: Every section must be scannable in seconds. Use straightforward language and clear visual buckets.
2. **Clinical Sophistication**: Evoke medical trust through precision alignment, ample whitespace, and intentional typography.
3. **Functional Minimalism**: Every flourish must serve a purpose. If it doesn't help the user make a decision or trust the brand, remove it.
4. **Airy Calm**: Maintain a light, "clean-room" atmosphere through neutral tones and subtle, non-distracting interactions.
